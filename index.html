Welcome to my homepage. I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College, working with Professor Anne Gelb. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Syracuse University in June 2026, where I was advised by Professor Lixin Shen. My dissertation, Image Restoration Regularized by Structured Sparsity Promoting Functions: Theory, Algorithms, and Applications, focuses on variational models, structured sparsity promoting regularization, Moreau envelope based methods, and optimization algorithms for image restoration. More broadly, my research interests include inverse problems, computational imaging, nonconvex optimization, and data driven approaches for reconstruction problems.
