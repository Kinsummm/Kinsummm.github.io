Welcome to my homepage. I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mathematics at Syracuse University. My research lies in optimization, inverse problems, image restoration, and structured sparsity promoting regularization. I am particularly interested in variational models, Moreau envelope based methods, and computational approaches for nonconvex optimization.

I also have teaching experience in calculus and probability and statistics courses, and I enjoy presenting mathematical ideas in a clear and accessible way.
